Introduction to the project

IDEA AND FIRST APPROACH:

We started the process by choosing between two projects, we had the opportunity to select one between the following: Choose one: 1. Design, draw, prepare and mill something with Shop Bot. Plan and document feeds and speeds, toolpaths, etc. for what was designed. Register the project and the results on a common website. Available will be 1500x1500mm of 18mm plywood. (will be updated later with more details). For example, interior design, furniture, wall art, frames 2. Design, draw, prepare and mill molds in wax with Roland SRM-20. Prepare toolpaths, both roughing and finishing. Register the project and the results on a common website. Read the MSDS and plan based on them if necessary. 15x7x4cm wax and silicone for mold making will be available. For example, a component, a toy, a container, hung on a wall. We chose the first one and the idea that won, at the end, has been a Minibar to ideate in Fusion 360 and then produce using the machine Shop Bot.

It took a long time to produce it, but the process has been very interesting. I personally enjoyed learning more about the machine Shop Bot. (Talking about the machine) CNC machines, or computer numerically controlled machines, are electro-mechanical devices that can manipulate tools around a varying number of axes, usually 3 or 5, with high precision per instruction from a computer program. CNC machining is one of two ways that generate a physical part from a computer design file and subtractive manufacturing (CNC machining) is the machining process in which solid piece of raw material is carved into a desired 3D geometrical shape and size by using a controlled material-removal process.
